Abstract: Community is the keyword of Michael's talk. Starting an Open Source project is easy. Getting new contributors for your project is a little bit harder, but still manageable. How many contributors will stick around for 2 month? 6 month? Years? Maintaining communities is the hardest part. I will show you a few tricks and tips for maintaining communities in this talk on the example of Mozilla.
